Welcome to the School of Business (SOB)

The school of Business at CLOU is where innovation meets excellence. Our school is dedicated to fostering a community of forward-thinking leaders who are passionate about shaping the future of Business. With a focus on experiential learnin, cutting-edge research, and global perspectives, our programs equip students with the knowledge, skills, and network to succeed in today's fast-paced business environment. Our aim is to develop responsible, enterpreneurial, and innovative thinkers who can drive positive change in their organisations and communities. With a strong commitment to academic excellence,industry engagement, and social responsibility, our school offers a transformative education that prepares students to thrive in their careers and make a lasting impact in the world through BUSINESS.
