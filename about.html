WELCOME NOTE

We are pleased with your decision to undertake tertiary studies at Chief Loyal University (CLOU). You are warmly welcome to our Centre of Excellence which offers value added teaching, research and public service. This website describes the range of academic programmes, which CLOU offers, and various activities undertaken as part of the University Mission and Vision.


In the past eleven years, CLOU has demonstrated a remarkable expansion despite many challenges. However, since challenges are the stimulus to success, CLOU has attempted to face them with unparalleled determination. Losing to challenges has never been part of CLOU vocabulary.


The Chief Loyal University provides a unique environment for teaching, learning, research, and public services to ensure a memorable and lifetime experience. Additionally, since the quality of student experience is as well a function of other factors in addition to academic pursuits, CLOU encourages students to engage in various activities of the student union by capitalising on the available sports and recreational facilities in the colleges. While at the university, students should cherish the motto “We shall solve our problems through dialogue”, and CLOU expects the motto to continue inspiring the university community in the future.


Chief Loyal University aims to ensure that each student get better experience in his or her field, which will rise the students perfomance even after school life whether in works that are associated with their courses or out of their courses. This will help them to know how to handle life matters after graduating in order to persue better living standards

OUR TEAM

CLOU has a strong team that is composed of distinguished scholars and accomplished professionals who bring a wealth of the experience, integrity, and vision to the institution. Under the strategic direction of our Vice Chancellor, the team is committed to upholding academic excellence, fostering research innovation, and maintaining the highest standards of governance. Their colleborative leadership ensures that the university continues to advance its mission of providing quality education and shaping future leaders of society.


With a strong dedication to institutional growth and student success, the leadership team continually implements policies and initiatives that promote inclusivity, accountability, and sustainable development. Their unwavering commitment to transparency and excellence inspires confidence across the university community, reinforcing the belief that our institution is guided by capable hands and clear visiom for the future


Beyond management, our leadership team serves as mentors, advocates and role models for our students. They are deeply committed to transparency, student welfare and continuous improvement in teaching and research. With a passion for nurturing future leaders, they inspire confidence, encourage creativity, and ensure that every decision made serves the best interests of our university community
