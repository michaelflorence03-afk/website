Welcome to the college of Education (COED)

The College of Education is the heart of academic excellence and innovation in teacher preparation. It is committed to developing professional educators who inspire, lead, and transform learning in schools and communities. Through its dynamic programs, students gain both pedagogical knowledge and practical experience for the modern classroom. Our faculty of experts ensures that every graduate is equipped with critical thinking, creativity, and leadership skills. The College continues to shape the future of education by promoting integrity, inclusivity, and lifelong learning.
